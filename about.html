About-Us

HairXterification is a brand that is well known for her touch to give a stunning look for her prospective customers. thus, rendering hair and other beautification services to their satisfaction. Moreover we render hair services such as weaving and fixing of wigs, braiding, ghana weaving and didi, crotchet fixing , even to the native hairstyle; such as plaiting (kiko and didi olowo). We also specialize in Wig making: from the range of long weaves to that of short styes using different methods and styles.

During the course of our services we also recommend hair care product for our customers, so as to facilitate healthy and rapid growth of their hair in order to bring them to full satisfaction and extraordinary beautification. Also, for the convenience of our customers, we render home services. Our company is situated in Kwara State, Ilorin,Tanke Odo University Road Balogun(Grace Villa) behind madam Segi hostel. Meanwhile our other branch is located at plot 9 APX estate off Odekun junction Orita Challenge, Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria.

Our Mission

Our mission is to take services to client door step, render home services at affordable cost yet effective, free hair treatment to our reliable customers(terms and conditions applied), booking of appointment to our prospective customers during weekends, hair services to students as subsidized rate. We are distinguished from others simply because, our customers stunning look is our greatest priority..
